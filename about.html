About Keep7

What is Keep7?

Keep7 is a free, browser-based tool for Magic: The Gathering players who want to test how their decks actually perform. Paste a decklist, draw opening hands, keep or mulligan, and see your stats build up over a session.

There are three modes:

  • Hand Test — Draw a 7-card opener. Keep or Mulligan. See land count, keep-rate and draw odds update in real time.
  • Goldfish — Step through turns 1–5. Play land drops and track how your mana develops.
  • Deck Race — Compare two decklists side-by-side across 20 simulated openers. Useful for choosing between two builds.

How it works

Keep7 runs entirely in your browser. When you paste a decklist and click "Test Opening Hand", the app calls the Scryfall API to fetch card data and images. That data is cached locally in your browser so subsequent sessions with the same deck are instant.

All shuffling and simulation is done client-side using the Fisher-Yates algorithm. No data leaves your browser beyond the Scryfall card name lookup.

Understanding the metrics

  • Keep Rate — What percentage of your opening hands you chose to keep.
  • Avg Lands — The average number of lands across all opening hands drawn this session.
  • 2-Land % / 3-Land % — How often your opener had exactly 2 or 3 lands.
  • T3 Land ~% — Estimated probability (hypergeometric) that you'll draw at least one more land in the next 3 draws, given the lands left in your deck.
  • Flood Risk / Screw Risk — (Deck Race) Percentage of simulated hands with 6+ lands (flood) or 0–1 lands (screw).

Privacy

Keep7 does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. There are no accounts, no analytics, and no tracking cookies.

The only external requests made are:

  • Scryfall API (api.scryfall.com) — to look up card names and images. Card names are sent as part of a POST request. No personal information is included.
  • MTGJSON (mtgjson.com) — to fetch random prebuilt decklists when you use "Random Web Deck". No personal information is sent.
  • Google Fonts — to load the Space Mono and DM Sans typefaces.

Decklist text and card cache data are stored in your browser's localStorage and are never sent to any server operated by Keep7.
